    Type Alias CRListState<T>

    Mutable CRList replica state.

    parentMap indexes live entries by UUIDv7. childrenMap indexes entries by predecessor to support deterministic flattening. tombstones records deleted UUIDv7 entries until they are garbage collected through acknowledgement frontiers.
